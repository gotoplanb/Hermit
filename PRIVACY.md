# Privacy Policy — Hermit SSH

**Last updated: March 16, 2026**

## Data Collection

Hermit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. There are no analytics, tracking, advertising, or telemetry of any kind.

## Data Stored on Your Device

- **SSH connection details** (hostnames, ports, usernames, session names) are stored in a JSON file in your iCloud Drive container. This data syncs across your devices via Apple's iCloud infrastructure. Hermit does not operate any servers — sync is handled entirely by Apple.
- **SSH private keys** are stored in the iOS Keychain on your device. Keys are never exported, transmitted, or included in iCloud backups.

## Third-Party Services

Hermit does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Super Whisper Integration

If you choose to use the optional Super Whisper voice transcription integration, Hermit opens the Super Whisper app via a URL scheme and receives transcribed text back. Hermit does not process audio or send audio data to any service. Super Whisper's own privacy policy governs its handling of your audio.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [github.com/gotoplanb/Hermit](https://github.com/gotoplanb/Hermit).
